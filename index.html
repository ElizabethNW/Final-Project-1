About Us

We are a team of qualified Life Coaches and Mental Health First Aiders, currently training as Counsellors in and around South Wales, including Cardiff. Our work is grounded in empathy, professionalism, and a commitment to creating a safe, supportive environment for every client.

As part of our training and ethical practice, some counselling sessions may be observed by a qualified supervisor. This helps ensure high‑quality support and reflective practice. We only proceed with your full consent, and your comfort and confidentiality remain our priority.

To make counselling more accessible, our trainee counsellor sessions are offered at a reduced rate starting from £20 per hour. Coaching sessions with fully qualified Coaches are available from £45 per hour.
